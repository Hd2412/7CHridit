Cricket

Cricket is a sport with a historical background that traces back to late 16th century England. It was officially recognized as a sporting activity in the 18th century and has since expanded its influence globally throughout the 19th and 20th centuries. International matches have been conducted since the 19th century, with formal Test cricket matches commencing in 1877. Presently, cricket ranks as the second most popular spectator sport in the world, following football (soccer). While the precise origins of cricket remain uncertain, it is believed that the sport may have been played as early as the 13th century.
